Privacy model

Built for people who cannot send work audio to the cloud.

Dictation runs locally on your Mac. The app asks for Microphone and Accessibility/Input Monitoring because every serious dictation app needs to listen and paste.

V0 does not ask for Screen Recording, Calendar, Contacts, or Files access. Diagnostics are local unless you choose to share them.
